School Vision

Making a dignified student population by emerging and polishing children's potentials.

School Mission

The Mission of school is to make future citizens who have skills and human qualities suitable for the 20th century and who can face challenges with self-confidence and self-strength.

Our Journey Through Time

1941

The Beginning

Established as a primary school on February 5th, 1941, marking the foundation of educational excellence in Ganthiriyagama, Anuradhapura.

1961

Upgraded to Maha Vidyalaya

On June 1st, the school was elevated to Mahinda Maha Vidyalaya status, expanding its educational scope.

2006

Navodya School Status

Achieved Navodya School status on April 1st, marking a significant milestone in our journey.

2023

National College

Proudly elevated to National College status on January 1st, recognizing our commitment to educational excellence.

Today, Ganthiriyagama National College stands as a beacon of educational excellence, dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders. Our institution continues to uphold its rich legacy while embracing modern educational practices.
